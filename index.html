Key Rules
Progressive overload
Add weight or 1 extra rep each week. Small increases compound massively over 14 weeks.
Reps in reserve (RIR)
Ph1: 3–4 reps from failure. Ph2: 2 reps. Ph3: 1–2 reps, some sets near-failure.
Rest between sets
Ph1: 60–90s. Ph2: 45–75s. Ph3: 45–60s. Use a phone timer.
Deload (Week 9)
50% of normal weight, same movements. Non-negotiable — recovery is where progress is made.
Form first, weight second
If form breaks, reduce the weight. Bad form = no results + injury risk.
Log weights in the Excel tracker · Aim for ~1.6–2g protein/kg bodyweight daily
