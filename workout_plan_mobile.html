PHASE 1 — Foundation
Key Rules
Progressive overload
Add weight or 1 extra rep each week. Small increases compound over 8 weeks.
Reps in reserve (RIR)
Phase 1: stop 3–4 reps before failure. Phase 2: stop 1–2 reps before failure.
Rest between sets
Phase 1: 60–90 seconds. Phase 2: 45–75 seconds. Use your phone timer.
Form first, weight second
If form breaks down, reduce the weight. Bad form = no results + injury risk.
Log weights in the Excel tracker · Aim for ~1.6–2g protein/kg bodyweight daily
